General Questions

We offer world-class embroidery digitizing, professional raster-to-vector art conversion, and premium custom patch manufacturing (including embroidered, woven, PVC rubber, leather, and chenille patches). We supply clean, high-precision production-ready files and physical products.

Placing an order is simple! Click any "Get Free Quote" button on our site to upload your logo or design artwork. Our design team will analyze your files and send you a digital proof and price estimate within a couple of hours. Once you approve, we begin production immediately.

For standard digitizing and vector conversion files, our turnaround is under 24 hours. Physical patch production and delivery typically take 7 to 14 business days, depending on the complexity of the design and order quantity. We also offer express options for rush orders.

Embroidery Digitizing

We supply all major commercial and home embroidery machine extensions including DST, PES, EXP, HUS, JEF, CND, EMB, and PDF worksheets. Unless specified otherwise, commercial files are sent in standard DST format.

Yes! We specialize in premium 3D Puff (raised) digitizing for caps, visors, and outerwear. We manually program thick underlays and clean satin columns to ensure the foam is sliced cleanly and the design stands out perfectly.

Absolutely. Our veteran digitizers have years of experience converting high-detail artwork, gradients, and intricate text into clean stitch paths. We optimize density and angles to prevent thread breaks, fabric puckering, and alignment shifts on commercial embroidery machines.

Vector Art

Yes. We manually trace low-resolution JPG, PNG, or hand-drawn raster artwork into clean, mathematical vector paths. This ensures your logo can scale to any size (from business cards to large billboards) without losing sharpness.

We deliver vector graphics in AI, EPS, PDF, SVG, and high-resolution transparent PNG. These industry-standard vector files are compatible with all modern editing software and commercial print systems.

Yes, 100%. Our vector conversions are built strictly using clean nodes, proper layer structures, and correct color models (CMYK or Pantone colors), making them instantly ready for screen printing, vinyl cutting, embroidery, or large-scale digital printing.

Custom Patches

We manufacture a wide range of premium custom patches: traditional high-thread-count Embroidered patches, micro-thread Woven patches for extreme detail, durable and waterproof molded PVC rubber patches, classic Leather patches, and fuzzy Chenille patches.

We believe in supporting businesses of all sizes, so we have no strict minimum order quantity (MOQ) for digital digitizing and vector files. For physical custom patches, our minimum order is just 10 pieces, making it easy to order samples or small team quantities.

Yes, we always provide a high-resolution digital mock-up for your review before physical production begins. For large patch orders, we can also provide a photograph of the first physical stitch-out or mold sample for final approval.

Pricing & Delivery

We offer simple, flat-rate pricing based on the size and placement of the design (such as left chest/caps versus full jacket backs) rather than counting stitches. This makes your budgeting highly predictable. Get a quote to see our competitive rates!

Our vector conversion starts at low flat rates, varying slightly depending on the complexity of the design (e.g., simple clean text logo vs. complex hand-drawn illustration with fine details). All quotes are transparent with no hidden fees.

Digital files (digitized embroidery maps and vector source files) are delivered securely via email or download link. Physical products like custom patches are shipped worldwide using reliable carriers like DHL, FedEx, and UPS, complete with tracking numbers.
