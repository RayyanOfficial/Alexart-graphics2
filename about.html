Who We Are

Mastering Stitches & Vector Art Since 2014

Alex Art & Graphics was founded in the US with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between creative visual designs and high-efficiency machinery. For years, custom embroiderers and printing shops have struggled with file errors, layout scaling, and thread breaks. We built our agency to fix that.

Our team of expert digitizers and vector artists works closely with clients to review stitch spacing, select borders, and outline clean vector shapes. Today, we handle volume ordering for commercial shops and single projects for local sports teams with the same high dedication to premium quality.

Work With Us

Our Mission

To provide commercial-grade, error-free embroidery digitizing, razor-sharp vector trace conversions, and high-fidelity custom patches. We aim to keep your sewing machines running at maximum speed with zero downtime, and your printing layouts crisp at any size.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for custom patch manufacturing and digitizing production, known for our hand-digitized precision, strict adherence to quality check procedures, and premium US agency support model.
